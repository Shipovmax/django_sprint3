Our Values

Self-expression. Our users should always have a chance to create something of their own and express their thoughts and feelings.

Diversity. We respect and welcome different opinions and cultures. Blogicum brings together people with very different backgrounds. Share your unique view of the world.

Creativity. Whatever your field may be, from literature to design to programming, our tools are built to help you reveal your talent in a simple and comfortable way.

Privacy. We keep your personal thoughts private. Share your information only with the people you choose. You decide how private your personal data should be.
